Quality Gates

These are the standards that decide whether the work keeps moving or loops back for fixes.

No cost cap
Ship Gate

What must be true before final PR

  • No unresolved Critical or High UI/UX defects in final screenshots.
  • Android high-traffic surfaces do not look like stock AnkiDroid.
  • Desktop does not collapse back into stock Anki on proof surfaces.
  • DMG and signed APK exist with checksums and launch proof.
  • REPORT.md and proof-packet.pdf point to screenshots/logs.
Loop Back

What forces another pass

  • Stock Anki blue, AnkiDroid copy, old palette soup, or test data residue appears in a final screenshot.
  • Reviewer or dashboard feels generic instead of CFA-first.
  • Readiness implies fake precision or hides insufficient evidence.
  • Packaging proof is source/dev-only instead of installable artifact proof.
  • Signing status is ambiguous or launch is not screenshot-backed.
