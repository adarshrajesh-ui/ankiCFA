CRITIQUE (senior-designer heuristic; GPT-4o vision unavailable — no OPENAI key)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
D11-1 (MAJOR): the eight actions were a flat, undifferentiated list — a
  dashboard, a report, three study modes, a settings dialog, and two account
  controls all as sibling rows with no grouping. Reads as a stock add-on dump,
  not a premium product's information architecture.
D11-2 (MINOR): no hover discoverability (no status tips).
D11-3 (—): both account entries always present — kept by design (a menu is the
  exhaustive surface; the D7 toolbar chip is the context-aware one).

FIX
---
Grouped into three labelled native sections via QMenu.addSection:
  Dashboard          -> CFA Home, Exam Readiness…
  Study modes        -> Study Ethics Minimal-Pairs, Study by Exam Priority,
                        Peak-on-Exam-Day (Deadline)…
  Settings & account -> AI Settings…, Connect to CFA Sync server,
                        Log out of Sync…
Section headers degrade to plain separators where section text isn't rendered.
Every command gained a concise setStatusTip for hover discoverability.
Structure-only change to setup_menu — no handler wiring changed, no cfa_style
token touched.
