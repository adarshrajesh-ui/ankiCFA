WHAT CHANGED (interaction)
--------------------------
The old multiple-choice "name the decisive fact" step is replaced by an
in-vignette tap-to-highlight: the learner taps the first + last word (or drags)
of the decisive phrase in the vignette they judge a VIOLATION. Grading is by
word-span overlap between the contiguous SELECTION and the authored GOLD phrase:

  CORRECT  : selection contains EVERY gold word AND |selection| <= cap
  SOMEWHAT : selection contains EVERY gold word BUT |selection| >  cap
  WRONG    : empty selection, or a gold word is missing
  cap = len(gold) + HIGHLIGHT_CAP_SLACK,  HIGHLIGHT_CAP_SLACK = 5

A pair is fully "correct" only when BOTH conform/violate judgments are right
AND the highlight grades "correct" (a "somewhat" highlight does NOT count), and
only when the highlight is made in the vignette that actually holds the phrase.

The tokenize / normalized_tokens / find_gold_indices / grade_highlight logic is
mirrored BYTE-FOR-BYTE across three places:
  - cfa/ethics_pairs/ethics_scoring.py            (Python scorer)
  - cfa/ethics_pairs/templates/front.html         (card template JS)
  - cfa/ethics_pairs/tests/js/highlight_logic.js  (Node copy for the cross-check)
test_highlight.py enforces both the JS<->JS copy match and the Python<->JS
behavioural agreement (run_matrix.js feeds a case matrix through the JS copy and
compares grades to the Python implementation).
